Virat Kohli (born November 5, 1988, Delhi, India) is an Indian international cricketer considered by many as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the game. Kohli plays for (and previously captained) the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) in the Indian Premier League (IPL).Throughout the tournament, Kohli struggled to find his form. In the final, he elevated his game to take his side to a competitive total, demonstrating why he is often called "King Kohli."
